Debugging 

What is Red dot ?
Red dot is a Break Point to stop the Debugging.

What is Debugging ?
Debugging will help us to identify that exact part of your code where the problem occurs.
it also help us to understand the flow of our code.


What is Stepover ?
step over will help us to move from current line to the next line only in same method.


During Debugging How do i go to that method?
Step into. is used to go to inside the method which is called.


What is Step Out ?
Step out is used to come out from that method and goes to from where it is called.


What is Resume program ?
it is used to execute the complete program.


What is Evaluate Expression?
Evaluate Expression will tell what this method is returning without even going to that method and checking.
this feature has only IntelliJ .


What is Quick fix ?
Quick fix is used to fix the bug or warning automatically.

IMPORTANT shortcut key FEATURES 

ALT + 6  is used to show the warnings.
ctrl + B is used to find out the method or variable where it is declared .
ctrl + F12 that will give you all the content like variables methods constructors means everything .
